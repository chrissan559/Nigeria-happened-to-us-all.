It is the output of a system working exactly as designed — for a small group that benefits from mass dependency, mass confusion, and mass compliance.

Root Causes & Exits

01
Miseducation
The curriculum trains people to seek jobs that don't exist. Credentials without leverage. Certification without the ability to create value independently.
Exit
Systems literacy. Learn how markets, capital, and leverage actually work. The curriculum is not the education — self-directed operator training is.
02
Regulatory Capture
Laws written by incumbents to block competition. You need capital to get permission to make capital. Formal operation becomes a trap for small operators.
Exit
Operate in markets where the tollbooth is optional or avoidable. Digital, cross-border, informal-but-structured. Don't fight the gate — go around it.
03
Infrastructure as Leverage
NEPA, roads, water — these are not failures. They are dependency mechanisms. The state has no incentive to fix what keeps people needing it.
Exit
Build infrastructure-independent income. Digital products, remote income, solar, generators as business assets — stop being held hostage by state failure.
04
Religion & Deferred Thinking
Faith weaponized into passivity. "God will provide" replaces planning. The pastor collecting your seed offering is running a sophisticated capital operation — and teaching you not to.
Exit
Separate faith from financial passivity. Plan, build, and act — then pray. The most effective operators in any spiritual tradition are also meticulous planners.
05
Tribal Identity Over Class Consciousness
People who share economic interests fight along ethnic lines instead. The extractors are post-tribal — they intermarry and share contracts across ethnic lines at the top.
Exit
Organize by economic interest, not identity. Build networks around value creation and shared incentives. The tribe that matters is the one building together.
06
Currency & Savings Trap
Inflation silently taxes naira savings at 25%+ annually. The average Nigerian holds naira in a bank earning 4%. That's not saving — it's a slow confiscation.
Exit
Store value in dollar, land, and business equity. Mirror exactly what the extracting class does. Stop being the person inflation happens to.
